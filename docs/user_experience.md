# üöó **2. Driving Modes Overview**

Veydra supports five primary modes.  
All modes share the same physical limits but adjust responsiveness, regen, steering weights, and safety system behaviors.

---

# **Mode 1: Comfort**
Everyday driving mode.

### Characteristics:
- Smooth throttle response  
- Moderate regen  
- Softer steering profile  
- Quieter cabin (adaptive acoustic management)  
- Balanced energy use  

Ideal for commuting and general travel.

---

# **Mode 2: Sport**
More responsive and dynamic.

### Characteristics:
- Sharper throttle mapping  
- Stronger regen for ‚Äúone-pedal‚Äù potential  
- More aggressive torque vectoring  
- Tighter steering feedback  
- Reduced stability intervention  

Sport mode maintains efficiency goals but prioritizes driver engagement.

---

# **Mode 3: Eco**
Maximizes range and energy efficiency.

### Characteristics:
- Soft throttle mapping  
- Highest regen efficiency  
- Reduced climate system load  
- Predictive energy management  
- Recommended speeds via eco-HUD cues  

Eco is not restrictive ‚Äî it simply optimizes the system.

---

# **Mode 4: Custom**
User-configurable mode.

Drivers can tune:
- Regen strength  
- Steering feel  
- Throttle response  
- Brake blending  
- Torque split (AWD model)  

Custom mode allows personalization without complexity.

---

# üö® **Mode 5: Defensive Mode**  
A unique Veydra feature designed for pre-emptive safety.

### **Purpose**
If the driver anticipates a dangerous situation ‚Äî heavy traffic, icy roads, a distracted driver behind them ‚Äî they can **manually engage Defensive Mode**.

### **Activation Results**
When engaged:
- Seat belts pre-tension  
- AeroBrake system primes for near-instant deployment  
- Extra safety thresholds are enabled in sensor fusion  
- Torque mapping becomes more conservative  
- Stability control becomes more sensitive  
- External hazards are highlighted in the HUD  

### **Predictive Capabilities**
Even before ABS or ESC intervene, Defensive Mode is readying the vehicle to react.
