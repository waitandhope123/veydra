# üöß **2. Smart Structural System**

Veydra employs an interconnected structure designed to absorb, redirect, and manage crash energy.

## **2.1 Side Sills**
High-strength, multi-cell extrusions that:
- Carry longitudinal impact loads  
- Prevent side intrusion  
- Route forces around the battery modules  

## **2.2 Crossmembers**
Front and rear crossmembers:
- Tie into the sills to form a rigid loop  
- Serve as anchor points for suspension and e-axles  
- Provide primary crash crush zones  

## **2.3 Controlled Crush Structures**
Crush regions are engineered to deform in specific patterns:
- Progressive collapse  
- Avoidance of battery boundaries  
- Consistent force levels for occupant protection  

## **2.4 Roof & Pillar System**
A/B/C pillars are:
- Reinforced for rollover protection  
- Integrated into load loops  
- Connected to side sills to avoid cabin deformation

# ü™´ **3. Battery Safety & Zonal Protection**

Veydra‚Äôs battery is the structural floor and thus receives special safety architecture.

### **3.1 Zonal Battery Layout**
The pack is divided into zones:
- Front zone  
- Mid zone  
- Rear zone  

Each zone has:
- Local contactors  
- Independent monitoring  
- Fault isolation capability  

### **3.2 Structural Isolation**
Between each battery zone:
- Physical separators  
- Thermal barriers  
- Crush-resistant enclosure walls  

### **3.3 Crash Energy Routing**
Crash structures direct forces:
- Away from cell modules  
- Through the sills, pillars, and crossmembers  
- Along engineered paths around the battery interior  

### **3.4 Venting Strategy**
Should thermal runaway occur:
- Gases vent downward and away  
- No vent paths directed toward the cabin  
- Pack enclosure routes flow to low-risk zones
